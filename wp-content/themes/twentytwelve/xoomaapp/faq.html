How do I benefit with Xooma Track & Go?

A: Xooma Track & Go is specially designed to track your intake of Xooma supplements by giving you timely reminders for products under ‘My Xooma products’

What are My Xooma products?

A: This are the supplements which you take and will be notified for. You can add them from ‘List of Xooma products’.

What is ‘List of Xooma products’?

A: This are the Xooma products. You have to click on ‘Add’ (Available under each product) to make them a part of ‘My Xooma products’. Once clicked on add the product is no longer available under ‘List of Xooma products’ .You get reminders for a product only when the product is added to ‘My Xooma Product’

How can I set reminders for product intake?

A: Reminder has to be set for each serving of a product. Fields for reminders get populated depending on the number of servings selected.You just have to choose a time for the reminder for each serving.Also by default the reminders will be on at app level (Available for mobile phones, under settings You have got two options from where you can set reminders a. While adding product(From ‘list of Xooma products’): The product is available to edit and hence reminders can be set from here b. Menu option on the card: Once the product has been added it is available under my product list.Each product has its card, wherein menu icon is available. From the menu icon select option edit product and reminders can be sent.

How do i consume X2O?

A: You have to be on homepage to accomplish this action.Click on ‘Hydrate’(on the X2O bottle) and you will be diverted to the consumption screen.You have to long press on the X2O bottle and the content in the bottle will keep decreasing.When the desired consuption level is reached release the mouse button, amount of your consumption will be displayed. You have to click on confirm to save the consumption.

How to I consume a supplement?

A: This action can be accomplished from the homepage.You have to just click on ‘Tap to take’ for any product and you will be diverted to the consumption screen.You can select the time you consume and hence save the consumption.

How do I add stock for a product?

A: You can manage your stock with the help of ‘inventory’. Each product has its own inventory. Here you can refill your inventory and also adjust your existing inventory. Steps to reach inventory screen are:
a.Go to ‘My Xooma products’
b.For each product card there is a menu option available. Click on it.
c.Select the inventory option

How do i manage my inventory ?

A: After stock for a product is added the inventory will keep reducing as per your consumption. You also have option to adjust your inventory(add/subtract number of supplements available) by using the slider in the inventory.

What happens when I ‘remove product’?

A: Once a product is removed is does not form a part of users ‘my product list’,hence no reminders will be given once a product is removed. You can add the product back from List of Xooma products,however all the previous consumptions of the product will be cleared.

Why do I have to update my measurements?

A: Measurements are required to track your progress.Updating the measurements daily helps us to show you how your body benefits with Xooma supplements with the help of progress charts.

What is progress chart?

A: Progress chart displays the measurements you update in graphical form.You have to select the part of the body and the time duration for which you want to see your progress for By default progress fro weight will be displayed.

What is the difference between the progress chart for weight and Bmi?

Why do I have to update my measurements?

A: Progress chart for weight is displayed according to the weight you enter whereas BMI (Body Mass Index) chart calculates your BMI depending on your measurements,and hence displays whether your BMI ratio is in the normal range or not.

Can I see my previous updated consumptions and measurements.?

A: Yes you can see your previous updated consumptions and measurements by using the history option.Each suppletion has its own history, Steps to view consumption history of any product :
a. Go to home
b. Go to the card of the desired product and click on the ‘menu’ option (Top right corner of the card) and select the option consumption history Steps to view Measurement history a. Go to the home page
b. Go to the progress chart card , click on menu on the card and select the option ‘measurement history’

How can i go to the consumption screen of any supplement?

A: You have to be on the home page for this.For X2O you have to click on ‘hydrate’ and for any other supplement you have to click on 'Tap to take'.

How can i get email notifications?

A: An option ‘Email alerts’ is available for the same.You have to put it on, to get email alerts. This is available under Menu>Settings>Email alerts.
